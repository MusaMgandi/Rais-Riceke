About RAIS

Our Journey to Excellence

RAIS is Kenya's premier rice supplier, dedicated to bringing you the highest quality rice at affordable prices. Founded in 2015, we've built strong relationships with local farmers across Kenya's rice-growing regions.

Our rice is carefully selected, processed, and packaged to maintain its natural goodness and nutritional value. We believe in sustainable farming practices and supporting local communities.
